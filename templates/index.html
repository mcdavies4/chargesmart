The smartest EV charger finder across the UK, US and Europe

Finding a free EV charging point should not be a guessing game. ChargeSmart uses artificial intelligence to analyse charging patterns across thousands of charging stations in the UK, United States, Norway, Germany, Netherlands, Sweden and France, predicting availability so you always know which charger to head to.

Whether you are an individual EV driver in London, Los Angeles, Berlin, Amsterdam or Oslo, or a fleet manager keeping dozens of electric vehicles charged and on the road, ChargeSmart gives you the intelligence to make smarter charging decisions.

In the UK: BP Pulse, Pod Point, Osprey, Gridserve and thousands of independent points. In the US: Tesla Supercharger, ChargePoint, EVgo, Blink and Electrify America. Across Europe: Ionity, Allego, Fastned, Recharge and national networks in Norway, Germany, Netherlands, Sweden and France. Free to use, no account required.

Frequently asked questions

How does ChargeSmart predict EV charger availability?
ChargeSmart uses a Random Forest machine learning model trained on over 8.5 million real data points from UK and US charging stations. It analyses time of day, day of week, location type, and charger capacity to predict how likely a charger is to be free when you arrive.
Does ChargeSmart work in the United States?
Yes! ChargeSmart covers the entire United States including the East Coast (New York, Boston, Washington DC), West Coast (Los Angeles, San Francisco, Seattle), Texas, and the Midwest (Chicago, Detroit). Simply enter your ZIP code or use GPS.
Does ChargeSmart work in Europe?
Yes! ChargeSmart now covers Norway, Germany, Netherlands, Sweden and France. These are among the highest EV adoption markets in the world. Simply enter your local postcode and ChargeSmart will find nearby chargers and predict availability. More European countries coming soon.
Which European charging networks does ChargeSmart cover?
ChargeSmart covers Ionity (pan-European), Allego (Netherlands and Germany), Fastned (Netherlands, Germany, France), Recharge (Norway and Sweden), EnBW (Germany), and thousands of local independent charging points across all covered countries.
Which charging networks does ChargeSmart cover?
In the UK: BP Pulse, Pod Point, Osprey, Gridserve, Ubitricity, InstaVolt and thousands of independent points. In the US: Tesla Supercharger, ChargePoint, EVgo, Blink, Electrify America, Volta and more.
Is ChargeSmart free to use?
Yes, ChargeSmart is completely free for individual EV drivers in both the UK and US. Fleet management features including the AI schedule planner are currently free during our early access period.
Can I install ChargeSmart on my phone?
Yes! ChargeSmart is a Progressive Web App (PWA). On Android, tap Install when prompted in Chrome. On iPhone, tap the Share button in Safari then Add to Home Screen. No App Store needed.
Can I use ChargeSmart for my EV delivery fleet?
Yes. ChargeSmart Fleet Mode is designed for logistics and delivery companies running EV fleets in the UK and US. Add your vehicles, set shift patterns, and let the AI generate an optimised charging schedule across your whole fleet.
